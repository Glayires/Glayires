About WI Design

Construction with Integrity

Based in the industrial hub of Volta Redonda, Rio de Janeiro, WI Design Projetos e Construcoes Ltda specializes in the nonresidential building sector. We understand that commercial and industrial facilities require a unique blend of durability, functionality, and compliance.

Under the leadership of Wiliton Jose De Oliveira, our firm bridges the gap between technical "Projetos" (Design) and on-site "Construcoes" (Construction). We eliminate communication barriers by managing the entire lifecycle of the build.

Whether it's a large-scale warehouse or a complex commercial retrofit, our team brings engineering rigor and safety consciousness to every square meter.
